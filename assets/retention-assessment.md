## SCORE INTERPRETATION

### 100-125: STRONG FOUNDATION ✅
Your teen has a strong foundation for retaining their faith. Continue what you're doing, but don't become complacent. The teen years are volatile.

**Priority Actions:**
- Maintain current practices
- Prepare for college transition (Module 4)
- Build Catholic peer connections
- Continue open dialogue

### 75-99: MODERATE RISK ⚠️
Your teen is in the danger zone where many drift. The foundation is there but needs strengthening.

**Priority Actions:**
- Complete all 5 modules of Teen Faith Lifesaver
- Focus on Module 1 (Retention Mindset) and Module 3 (Environment Design)
- Increase quality conversations about faith
- Address weak areas identified in assessment

### 50-74: HIGH RISK 🚨
Warning signs are present. Your teen is at significant risk of leaving the Church.

**Priority Actions:**
- Immediate intervention using Module 5 (Crisis Protocol)
- Do not delay—act within days, not weeks
- Consider professional help (spiritual director, counselor)
- Rebuild relationship before addressing faith directly

### 0-49: CRISIS MODE 🔴
Your teen is in crisis. They may have already left the faith or be on the verge.

**Priority Actions:**
- Crisis Protocol (Module 5) immediately
- Do not argue or lecture—rebuild connection first
- Seek professional help immediately
- Focus on relationship, not religious performance
- Pray. A lot.
