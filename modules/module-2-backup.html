Pre-Bunking: The Vaccine for Cultural Lies

The culture doesn't attack faith directly. It sneaks in through entertainment, social media, and "just asking questions." By the time your teen hears the lie, it's already too late—unless you've pre-bunked it.

Inoculation works by exposing your teen to weakened versions of cultural arguments before they encounter the full-strength versions in the wild. This module shows you exactly how to do it without creating cynicism.

Apologetics That Actually Stick

Most apologetics training fails because it's information overload. Your teen doesn't need to win debates; they need to survive them. We focus on the five questions every teen will face:

  • Why does God allow suffering? — The question behind most departures
  • What about science and evolution? — The false conflict that destroys faith
  • Why is the Church against [X]? — The morality questions they'll face daily
  • How do you know God exists? — The foundation everything else rests on
  • Why Catholic and not just Christian? — The denominational confusion

Handling Doubt Without Panic

When your teen expresses doubt, your reaction determines everything. Panic pushes them away. Confidence invites them closer. This module gives you the exact framework for turning doubt into deeper faith.

Key Takeaways

  • Pre-bunking exposes lies before they become beliefs
  • Focus on the 5 questions every teen actually faces
  • Your reaction to doubt matters more than your answers
  • Apologetics should build confidence, not arrogance
  • The goal is resilience, not winning arguments
